About Us

Our History

Started in 2020,Pick Your Pickle quickly established itself as a 100% trusted brand for home-made pickles. With its unique taste and quality that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in India.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Corona Pandemic. Our CEO, Meghana, felt that a lot of people were stuck alone at homes or hostels, away from home-made food. Although regular food can be ordered online, the sale of home-made,fresh and safe pickle was a niche unexplored in the market. This led to the idea of 'Pick Your Pickle', the most trusted online pickle shop.
